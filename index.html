hello,
I'm Oli. I'm a self-taught, independent, Wellington-based creative specialising in photography and graphic design. I'm currently in my first year of studying a Bachelor of Communicative Design at the Massey University College of Creative Arts. I've been making art ever since I figured out what crayons were.
photography
Since the first time I picked up a proper camera, my primary interest has been travel photography. I'm always looking to expand my horizons. In the past year or so, I'm also starting to dip into the world of portrait photography - so I guess you could say I like my work to comprise of pretty people in pretty places.
